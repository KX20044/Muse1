What is Muse?

Muse is an all-inclusive holographic companion, using personal memory archives, emotion-tracing, and neural pattern recognition to collect traits, mannerisms and speech from you to offer high functioning predictive care routines in a groundbreaking new manner.

Through those you love and miss.

Muse accesses your personal archives to imitate a singular persona that reduces stress and offers familiar comfort. For those living alone, this offers a new way of living. To reimagine the past at prime.

Here at GENWELL, we pride ourselves on our innovation and boundary-breaking research and development to help you live well in your prime. Our home eldercare system relieves the burden on our heavily overworked healthcare industry.
